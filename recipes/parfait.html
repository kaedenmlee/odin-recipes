Yougurt Parfait

This yogurt parfait is very simple and enjoyable. It a great thing to make in the morning as it will start your day off right. It has nutritional value and also tastes amazing. This parfait will become a part of your daily routine.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla yogurt
  • 1 cup granola
  • 8 blackberries

Steps

  1. Layer 1 cup yogurt, 1/2 cup granola, and 4 blackberries in a large glass; repeat layers.
